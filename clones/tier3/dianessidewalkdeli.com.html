Diane's Sidewalk Deli

Diane’s Sidewalk Deli is a family owned and operated business that has been serving customers at the Chesterbrook Shopping Center for over 27 years.

Whether it’s eat-in, take-out, or catering, our portions are always more than generous and reflect the true traditions of a New York Style Deli. Our award winning food is prepared onsite daily so that customers will receive only the highest quality meals possible.

We are open 7 days a week for breakfast and lunch .

We take pride in bringing the traditional style of the New York Deli to our area. With generous portions, all of our dishes are prepared with the freshest ingredients on the spot. From soups, salads, and sandwiches to chicken tenders, mozzarella sticks, and burgers we have you covered. During these difficult economic times we are offering our dine-in customers daily lunch specials Monday through Thursday for only $8.95. These specials range from some of our most popular sandwiches to your selection of pick twos containing soups, salads, and sandwiches. Our family atmosphere and good food will have you coming back again and again.
